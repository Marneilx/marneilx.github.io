Happy 4th Monthsary, Bebu!

I am writing this letter with a heavy heart, regretting my behavior last night. I didn't mean to hurt you, babe. I was just so upset and I allowed my emotions to overpower my judgement, and I understand its negative impact on us. I apologize for being so thoughtless and not considering your feelings.
I am genuinely sorry, babe. I miss you and can't stand the thought of us not talking because of my mistake.

I cannot help but cry as I write this letter, desperately seeking your forgiveness. I hope that you will forgive me, babe.

It's been four months, and my love for you is as strong as ever. We've had disagreements and misunderstandings, but I'm always committed to us. Thank you for loving me as I am, even though I am sometimes hot-headed. Always remember that our petty fights will never take over our love.

I hope and wish that our love continues to flourish, overcoming any obstacles in our path.

I love you, babe. I can't wait to see you.

Yours always and forever,

Marneil
