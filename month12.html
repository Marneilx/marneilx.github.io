Dear Marvie

Dear Marvie,

Today would have been our first anniversary, and I couldn’t let the day pass without acknowledging the significance it holds.

We spent a lot of time looking forward to this day, planning to finally meet in person after a year of long-distance.

These past two months apart have given me a lot to think about.

I miss you.

I’ve realized that I still care about you a lot and I’d like to see if we can start over.

You deserve all the happiness and success in the world, and I truly hope you find joy in whatever you do.

Take care, and maybe we can make some new memories together. Until then, I’ll hold onto the positive memories.

Yours always and forever,

Marneil
